MediaRoom ("MediaRoom," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices you have when you use our website, rooms, chat features, uploads, and related services (the "Service").

We built MediaRoom to be simple and respectful. We do not sell personal information. We do not use personal information for behavioral advertising or profiling without consent. We collect and use information mainly to operate the Service, keep it secure, support the features you choose to use, and improve reliability.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information you provide directly

We may collect information you provide when you:

  • create an account
  • sign in using your email address and password
  • create or join a room
  • send chat messages
  • upload avatars, images, files, or other content
  • contact us for support or send us feedback

This information may include:

  • email address
  • encrypted or hashed authentication credentials
  • display name, username, or profile information
  • avatar or profile image
  • room names or room settings you create
  • chat messages and shared content
  • uploaded images, files, or media
  • communications you send to us

B. Information collected automatically

When you use the Service, we may automatically collect certain technical and usage information, such as:

  • IP address
  • browser type and version
  • device type and operating system
  • approximate location derived from IP address
  • referring page and pages viewed
  • dates, times, and duration of sessions
  • room participation metadata
  • log files, crash reports, diagnostics, and performance data
  • cookie and similar technology data

C. Information visible to other users

Some information is meant to be shared with other users depending on how you use the Service. This may include:

  • your display name
  • avatar or profile image
  • room presence or participation
  • chat messages
  • files, images, or content you intentionally share in a room

If you post, upload, or share content in a public room, that content may be visible to other users in that room. If you share content in a private room, visibility is limited to permitted participants, but you should still use care when sharing personal or sensitive information.

2. How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

  • create and manage user accounts
  • authenticate users and maintain account security
  • provide public and private room functionality
  • enable chat, uploads, and shared room features
  • store and display avatars, images, and files
  • operate embedded or linked media features
  • monitor system health, troubleshoot bugs, and improve performance
  • prevent spam, fraud, harassment, abuse, and other misuse
  • respond to support requests and communications
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies

We do not sell personal information.

We do not share personal information with third parties for their own direct marketing purposes.

We do not use personal information for targeted advertising or behavioral profiling without consent.

3. Cookies, Authentication, Analytics, and Logs

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • keep you signed in
  • remember preferences or session settings
  • maintain security
  • understand usage patterns
  • measure performance and reliability
  • improve the Service

We also use authentication tools, analytics tools, and database or server logs to operate and secure the Service.

These technologies may collect information such as session identifiers, device/browser information, page interactions, and technical event data.

You can usually manage cookies through your browser settings, though disabling some cookies may affect login or Service functionality.

When you interact with third-party embedded content or links, those third parties may collect information according to their own privacy policies and terms. We do not control the privacy practices of third-party services.

We may also use third-party providers for services such as:

  • hosting

We share information with these providers only as reasonably necessary for them to perform services on our behalf.

5. Public Rooms, Private Rooms, and Chat

MediaRoom includes both public and private rooms.

  • Public rooms: content you share there may be visible to other users in that room.
  • Private rooms: content is intended to be accessible only to invited or authorized participants.

Chat messages, uploads, usernames, and activity within rooms may be visible to room participants and may be stored as part of the normal operation of the Service.

Please do not share sensitive personal information in rooms or chat unless you are comfortable doing so.
